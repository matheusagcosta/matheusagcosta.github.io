Creativity has been part of my life since I was a little kid, drawing dogs and spaceships on paper.

Shifting to the web, I could learn the concept of iteration and how things could constantly be improved by getting feedback.

As a multidisciplinary designer and systems thinker, I aim to speak machine, not code, and translate that to the best user experience, where communication is the key to making our lives easier.
